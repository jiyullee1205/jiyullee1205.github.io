My Creative Writing & Storybook Journey

During the pandemic, I spent a lot of time rewatching the Harry Potter movies. Inspired by the stories, I came up with my own idea and decided to turn it into a book. I wrote the entire story in just one week, spending hours each day imagining characters and building my own world.

The publishing process took around six months, as I worked with a teacher who created the illustrations. I was actively involved in choosing the style of drawings and reviewing each page to make sure it matched my vision. Seeing my story come to life as a real book was one of the most exciting experiences I have ever had. It taught me the power of imagination, discipline, and collaboration.
