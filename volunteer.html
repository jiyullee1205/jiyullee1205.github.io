My Service and Volunteering Journey

Serving my community has always been important to me. From third grade until sixth grade, I regularly helped as an altar server at church. Even when services started very early in the morning, I felt proud to support and be part of something meaningful.

Beyond church, I also took part in elderly care activities, where I learned patience, kindness, and responsibility. At the Gwacheon Science Museum, I had the chance to connect my love for STEM with service by helping visitors engage with science in fun and educational ways.

These experiences taught me that volunteering is not just about giving time but about learning, growing, and making a positive impact on others.
