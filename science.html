My Science and Math Journey

My love for science started when I was young, exploring nature, reading science books, and doing small experiments at home. Later, I joined after-school programs where I learned the basics of robotics and coding. Building robots and writing programs with Arduino and Python helped me see how math and science connect to real-world technology.

I also enjoyed creating hands-on experiments and volunteering at the Gwacheon Science Museum, where I was able to share my passion with others. Competing in math contests and solving challenging problems gave me confidence and encouraged me to keep improving. Science and math have taught me to be curious, creative, and determined when facing new challenges.
