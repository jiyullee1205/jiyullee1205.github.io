My Adventures and Experiences

Adventures have always been a way for me to challenge myself and explore the world. One of my most memorable achievements was climbing Hallasan, the highest mountain in Korea, before a family trip to Jeju. I wanted to prove to myself that I could take on a challenge and succeed.

Every summer, I also camp with my grandfather at Jirisan. These trips taught me independence and built a special connection with nature and family. Traveling abroad has been equally meaningful, whether exploring New Zealand and Hawaii or visiting the United Nations in New York before participating in Model United Nations.

From outdoor sports like basketball and hiking to cultural and academic travel, each experience has shaped how I see the world and inspired me to keep learning and growing.
