My Music & Piano Journey

Piano has always been my way of exploring creativity. Instead of following a traditional path, I chose to focus on the songs and themes that inspired me. One of my proudest achievements was teaching myself to play the theme from “Pirates of the Caribbean,” which showed me that with patience and practice, I could play the music I love.

I also enjoy watching concerts and performances that inspire me to keep practicing. Piano is not just about playing notes but about expressing emotions and connecting with stories through music.
